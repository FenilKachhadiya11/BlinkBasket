Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

We strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not satisfied, you can return items under the following conditions:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and include all accessories or tags.
  • Items that are damaged or missing parts due to customer mishandling are not eligible for returns.

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund. Refunds are issued based on the following conditions:

  • If approved, refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part.
  • For exchanges, the replacement item will be shipped once the returned item is inspected.

How to Initiate a Return

To return an item, follow these steps:

  • Contact our support team at blinkbasketcustomercare@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.
  • We will provide a return authorization and shipping instructions.
  • Ship the item to the address provided. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Note: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I return a sale item?

A: Sale items are eligible for return unless stated otherwise on the product page.

Q: What should I do if I receive a damaged item?

A: Contact our support team within 7 days of receiving the item, and we will assist you with a replacement or refund.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team at blinkbasketcustomercare@gmail.com.
