Shipping Information

Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery options for your convenience:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total order amount and delivery location:

  • Orders over $50: Free Standard Shipping
  • Orders under $50: $5.99 for Standard Shipping
  • Express Shipping: $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping: $19.99

Estimated Delivery Times

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery times depend on the shipping option you select:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Note: Delivery times may vary due to holidays, weather conditions, or unforeseen circumstances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yes, tracking details will be emailed to you once your order is shipped.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: Currently, we only ship within India. We are working on expanding our services.
