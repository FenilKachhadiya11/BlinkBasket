Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to BlinkBasket. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Personal Information: When you register, make a purchase, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, payment information, etc.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you use our website, such as pages visited, clicks, and other actions you take.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience on our website by tracking preferences and providing personalized content.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process your orders and deliver products and services
  • To improve our website and services based on your feedback
  • To send promotional emails, newsletters, or other marketing communications with your consent
  • To respond to customer service inquiries and support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We use encryption technologies, secure servers, and other safeguards to ensure the confidentiality of your information.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with the following:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third parties who provide services like payment processing, order fulfillment, and email marketing.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your information to government authorities or legal entities to comply with regulations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information
  • Request the deletion of your data, subject to certain limitations
  • Opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions
  • Withdraw your consent for the use of cookies and tracking technologies

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
